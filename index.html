About Me

Hello! I am a researcher and engineer at Apple, working on privacy-preserving machine learning. I completed my Ph.D. in Computer Science at Cornell University (Cornell Tech) with Prof. Vitaly Shmatikov. Before that, I received my bachelor's degree at Emory University, where I worked closely with Prof. Ymir Vigfusson and Prof. Lee Cooper on some fun real world deep learning application projects.
